Elements HR Services - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What industries do you serve?
A: We serve all industries including IT, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, education, hospitality, and startups. Our solutions are customizable for any business size.

Q: What is the minimum number of employees required to use your services?
A: We work with companies of all sizes, from startups with 5 employees to large enterprises with 5000+ employees. There is no minimum requirement.

Q: How quickly can you start processing our payroll?
A: We can onboard your company within 3-5 working days. We need employee data, salary structure, and statutory registration details to begin.

Q: Do you handle PF and ESI registration for new companies?
A: Yes, we handle fresh PF and ESI registration. The process takes 7-10 working days. We manage the entire process including documentation.

Q: What is your recruitment guarantee period?
A: We offer a 90-day replacement guarantee for all permanent placements. If the candidate leaves within 90 days, we provide a free replacement.

Q: Can you handle payroll for employees in multiple states?
A: Yes, we manage multi-state payroll with state-specific compliance including Professional Tax, Labour Welfare Fund, and state-specific labour laws.

Q: Do you provide POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) compliance?
A: Yes, we help companies set up Internal Complaints Committee (ICC), draft POSH policy, conduct awareness training, and handle annual compliance filing.

Q: What software do you use for payroll processing?
A: We use industry-standard HRMS platforms and can integrate with your existing systems. We also provide our own cloud-based employee self-service portal.

Q: How do you ensure data security?
A: All employee data is stored on encrypted servers with role-based access control. We are ISO 27001 compliant and follow strict data protection protocols.

Q: What is your pricing model?
A: We offer flexible pricing based on the number of employees and services selected. Contact us for a customized quote. Basic payroll starts at ₹50/employee/month.

Q: Can I cancel the service anytime?
A: Yes, we require a 30-day notice period for service cancellation. All pending statutory filings will be completed before handover.

Q: Do you provide employee self-service portals?
A: Yes, employees can access their payslips, tax documents, leave balance, and attendance records through our online portal and mobile app.
