Elements HR Services - Service Policies

Onboarding Process:
1. Initial consultation and requirement gathering (Day 1)
2. Service agreement signing and documentation (Day 2)
3. Data collection - employee details, salary structure (Day 2-3)
4. System setup and configuration (Day 3-4)
5. Parallel payroll run for verification (Day 4-5)
6. Go-live with full service (Day 5 onwards)

Service Level Agreement (SLA):
- Payroll processing: Completed by 28th of every month
- Statutory filings: Filed before due dates (PF by 15th, ESI by 15th)
- Query resolution: Within 4 working hours
- Recruitment shortlisting: First batch within 5 working days
- Background verification: Report within 5-7 working days

Payment Terms:
- Monthly billing cycle
- Payment due within 15 days of invoice
- Accepted methods: Bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS), cheque, UPI
- Late payment: 1.5% interest per month after due date

Confidentiality:
- All client data is treated as strictly confidential
- NDA signed before engagement begins
- Employee data is never shared with third parties
- Data retention: 7 years as per statutory requirements
- Data deletion: Upon request after statutory retention period

Escalation Matrix:
- Level 1: Dedicated Account Manager (response within 4 hours)
- Level 2: Operations Head (response within 8 hours)
- Level 3: Director (response within 24 hours)
- Emergency: Direct hotline for critical payroll/compliance issues

Termination Policy:
- 30-day notice period required from either party
- All pending statutory filings completed before handover
- Complete data handover in standard format
- No exit charges for contracts over 6 months
